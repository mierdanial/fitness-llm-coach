Beginner strength training should focus on compound exercises such as squats, push-ups, and rows.

Training frequency:
Beginners should train 3 days per week with rest days in between sessions.

Typical repetition ranges:
Strength: 4-6 reps
Hypertrophy: 8-12 reps
Endurance: 12-15 reps

Rest intervals:
Strength: 2-3 minutes
Hypertrophy: 60-90 seconds

Progressive overload means gradually increasing weight, repetitions, or training volume over time.

Common beginner exercises:
Squat
Push-up
Dumbbell Row
Plank
Lunges
