TOPIC: Progressive Overload
==============================
Progressive overload is the gradual increase of stress placed on the body during exercise.
To build muscle and strength you must progressively increase weight, reps, sets, or intensity.

Rules:
- Increase load by 2.5–5% when all sets are completed with good form
- Beginners: progress weekly
- Intermediate: every 1–2 weeks
- Advanced: every 3–4 weeks

Methods:
- Increase weight
- Increase reps
- Increase sets
- Reduce rest time
- Improve range of motion
